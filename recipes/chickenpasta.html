Chicken Pasta Recipes

Description:

The chicken pasta is a balance of carbs and protein. There is the white chicken breast full of protein for the muscles and then the carbs that are also battling for territory. This recipe is full of great taste but not to waste on sauce. Thats about it.

Ingredients:

  • Banza Penne Noodles
  • Tyson Chicken Breast in a can
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onions
  • 3 Tablespoons of virgin oil

Instructions:

  1. Make the noodles as instructed. A good tip is bring the water to a boil and then add the noodles before taking them out in 8 minutes
  2. Open the can of chicken and cook lightly on a heated oiled skillet. Add any spices or seasoning to the chicken early on so that it has the favor sooner than later.
  3. Add any onions or sauces here/li>
  4. Take the meat and noodles out before combining
  5. Enjoy!
