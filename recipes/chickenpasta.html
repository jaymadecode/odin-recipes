Chicken Pasta Recipes

Description:

The chicken pasta is a balance of carbs and protein. There is the white chicken breast full of protein for the muscles and then the carbs that are also battling for territory. This recipe is full of great taste but not to waste on sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Banza Penne Noodles
  • Tyson Chicken Breast in a can
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup of chopped onions
  • 3 Tablespoons of virgin oil
