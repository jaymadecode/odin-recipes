Mug Pancake

Description:

Pancake in a mug is literally exactly what it's named. This is a quick and short recipe for those that want breakfast on the go or just want something quick. I highly suggest not putting a whole slab of butter on the pancake but to each their own.

Ingredients:

  • Two cups of Protein Pancake Mix
  • 1/4 cup of Almond Milk
  • 1/4 cup of water
