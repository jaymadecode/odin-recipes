Mug Pancake

Description:

Pancake in a mug is literally exactly what it's named. This is a quick and short recipe for those that want breakfast on the go or just want something quick. I highly suggest not putting a whole slab of butter on the pancake but to each their own.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of Protein Pancake Mix
  • 1/4 cup of Almond Milk
  • 1/4 cup of water

Instructions:

  1. Get a coffee mug, anyone of your choice
  2. Put the 1/2 cup of protein pancake mix into the mug
  3. Mix the Almond Milk
  4. Add the water
  5. Stir until the batter is not as lumpy. Try to make sure ther eis no visible powder at the bottom of the mug
  6. Put the mug in the microwave for 2 minutes.
  7. Take it out when there is no visible 'wet' spots on the surface!
  8. Enjoy!
