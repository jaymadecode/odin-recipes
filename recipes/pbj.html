Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwhich Recipe

Description:

The Peanut butter and Jelly sandwhich is a delicate recipe. Loved by millions and hated by some, there is no telling who will make this sandwhich. It has the most basic of ingredients and yet it is the cause of chaos and love. Peace and War. The PB&J knows no boundaries of man or God.

Ingredients:

  • Two slices of Sara's Delightful white Bread
  • 2 Tablespoons of Peanut Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon of Grape Jelly

Instructions:

  1. Get a plate
  2. Lay the two slices of bread down next to each other on the plate
  3. Get a butter knife before scooping a bit of peanut butter on there. Using the tablespoon measurer can help for exact measurements
  4. Slab the peanut butter onto the left slice of bread
  5. Take the knife and wipe it off first before doing the same with the grape jelly but putting the slab of jelly onto the right slice of bread
  6. Press the two slices of bread together so that the peanutbutter and the jelly mix or are in the middle.
  7. Enjoy!
